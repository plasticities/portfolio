Helvetica No More

This poster was created for the Rainer Erich Scheichelbauer conference at Concordia University on February 26, 2014. Rainer Erich Scheichelbauer is an established typographer with many typefaces under his belt. This poster was to be created to explain the conferenceâ€™s topic at hand but with only the use of typography. The creative choices done here were to show that the Helvetica typeface has lost its way amongst designers, as well as to promote the use of other great typefaces. The colour scheme used here, complimented the whole aesthetic of the poster in accomplishing a well thought out poster.
