Web Design

File-sharing

This website was made to illustrate the rich history of the file-sharing movement that was created on the Internet. Along each page was a brief description of the innovative ideas that were born in order to promote and encourage the notion of sharing media.

Let me see

Each page has a looping gif created to illustrate these ideas in a flat illustrative way. These gifs were created to accompany and enhance the informative aspect of the website, allowing for the viewer to be immersed in the overall experience. The website was created to be concise and informative in an aesthetically pleasing way.
