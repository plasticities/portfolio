Wonderland

This dichotomy was created as an appropriation of the classic Alice in Wonderland tale by Lewis Carroll. This appropriation used the playing cards metaphor of the tale to create symbolism and instant recognition of the said tale. By using imagery found in playing cards, and appropriating it to the characters in Alice in Wonderland,it creates a distinctive narrative and aesthetic to the poster.

And by using a dichotomy dynamic, it creates a stronger narrative, as opposed to only one; they work to compliment each other. The details in the two works provide meaning and symbolism and showcases two key plot points in the story.
