Sun Wukong

This motion graphics video was created to illustrate the ancient tale of Sun Wukong, also known as the Monkey King. This retelling of the story was made to showcase my illustration skillset and add another layer to it, by animating it. Each visual was drawn out beforehand, with every key setting in mind. Then, it was animated to enhance the story telling and capturing the viewer’s attention. The overall aesthetic choice was to emulate the story’s initial illustrative aspect. The animations themselves was made the key feature to tell the story, letting the visuals carry the story along. This video was made to showcase the illustrations and its story.
